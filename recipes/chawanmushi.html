Egg Custard Chawanmushi

A homey cup of egg-custard chawanmushi!

Great as a side dish or a snack on a cold day.

Ingredients

  • 4x Eggs
  • 2x Cups Strong Dashi
  • 1x teaspoon
  • 1x Shoyu/Japanese Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon Sugar
  • Optional Garnish Veggies like carrots, mushrooms, green onions, kamaboko, pine nuts for tecture etc.
  1. Gently beat eggs in a 1 quart measuring cup. Try to make sure eggs do not froth.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine dashi, mirin, shoyu, salt and sugar.
  3. Gently add eggs to dashi mixture and stir to combine.
  4. Pour egg-dashi mixture through a strainer back into the quart measuring cup.
  5. Divide garnish veggies evenly between four chawanmushi bowls/tea cups, cover, and steam over low heat for 12-15 minutes.
  6. Done. Careful, hot.
