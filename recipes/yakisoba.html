Tuskiji Yakisoba

Okay, this isn't a homecooking style dish. This is street food.

Great for when you're bored with regular yakisoba packets and want to augment the flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1x-2x Yakisoba Package/kit.
  • Thin meat slices like bacon, chicken, etc.
  • Ground ebi/shrimp flakes
  • Cabbage
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Yaki-soba sauce
  • Bonito Flakes
  • Nori Flakes
  • Optional: 1x-2x Eggs
  • Optional: Japanese mayo
  1. Meat, cabbage, bean sprouts on pan. Cook for 3-5 min
  2. 1-2 Yaki-soba noodle packs on pan, spray with water and cover to steam.
  3. (Optional) Fry eggs.
  4. Ebi-flakes onto noodles, then put over meat to mix flavors.
  5. Add yaki-soba sauce/packet and mix everything together.
  6. If egg, put on top.
  7. Bonito and nori flakes on top. Use Japanese mayo too if you want.
  8. Done.
